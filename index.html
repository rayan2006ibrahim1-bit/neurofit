Frequently asked questions

Do I need a wearable?

No. The app works without one for logging and coaching. A watch, ring, or band simply unlocks deeper insights.

What is CNS fatigue?

It’s stress on the central nervous system from training and life load. Identifying it early helps prevent plateaus and injury.

How is my data protected?

We use encryption in transit and at rest, strict access controls, and clear privacy options inside the app.
