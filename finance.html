Economics

Unit Economics & Key Metrics

Strong unit economics are the foundation of our business model. We achieve an 8:1 LTV:CAC ratio through high retention and efficient customer acquisition.

Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

$45
Blended CAC across all channels

CAC Breakdown by Channel

Note: CAC decreases over time as organic channels scale and brand awareness grows

Lifetime Value (LTV)

$360
Average customer lifetime value

LTV Calculation

Average Revenue per User (ARPU) $20/month
Average Customer Lifespan 18 months
Gross Margin 85%
LTV = ARPU × Lifespan × Margin $360
LTV by Subscription Tier
Premium ($19.99/mo) $306 LTV
Elite ($49.99/mo) $765 LTV

LTV:CAC Ratio

8:1
Exceptional unit economics

Industry Benchmarks

  • 3:1 - Minimum viable for SaaS
  • 5:1 - Good SaaS business
  • 8:1 - Excellent (VitaNexus target)
  • 10:1+ - World-class

CAC Payback Period

5.6 months to recover customer acquisition cost

CAC ($45) ÷ (ARPU ($20) × Margin (85%)) = 2.6 months

Risk Management

Financial Risks & Mitigation

Burn Rate Risk

High Impact

Risk: Running out of cash before achieving profitability or next funding round.

Mitigation Strategies:
  • Conservative 17-month runway from Seed round
  • Monthly cash flow monitoring and quarterly budget reviews
  • Flexible cost structure with ability to reduce marketing spend
  • Series A raised at Month 12, well before cash depletion
  • Contingency plan to extend runway by 6 months if needed

Customer Acquisition Cost

Medium Impact

Risk: CAC increases beyond projections, making unit economics unsustainable.

Mitigation Strategies:
  • Diversified acquisition channels reduce dependency on any single source
  • Strong referral program (20% of users) with low CAC ($8)
  • Content marketing and SEO for organic growth
  • Continuous A/B testing to optimize conversion rates
  • Target CAC of $45 with 30% buffer in financial model

Churn Rate

High Impact

Risk: Higher than expected churn reduces LTV and profitability.

Mitigation Strategies:
  • Comprehensive onboarding program to drive early engagement
  • AI personalization increases relevance and retention
  • Social features and community building create switching costs
  • Proactive churn prediction and intervention system
  • Target 75% monthly retention (conservative vs. 80% industry leaders)

Funding Risk

Medium Impact

Risk: Difficulty raising Series A or unfavorable terms in down market.

Mitigation Strategies:
  • Clear milestones and KPIs to demonstrate traction
  • Multiple revenue streams reduce single-point dependency
  • Path to profitability by Month 24 reduces funding dependency
  • Strong unit economics (8:1 LTV:CAC) attractive in any market
  • Early relationship building with Series A investors

Revenue Concentration

Low Impact

Risk: Over-reliance on B2C subscriptions limits growth and increases vulnerability.

Mitigation Strategies:
  • B2B corporate wellness (9% of revenue by Year 5)
  • Marketplace commission (6% of revenue)
  • Affiliate revenue (5% of revenue)
  • Brand partnerships and data licensing (10% combined)
  • Geographic diversification across multiple markets

Pricing Pressure

Low Impact

Risk: Competitive pressure forces price reductions, impacting margins.

Mitigation Strategies:
  • Premium positioning justified by AI technology and results
  • High switching costs through personalization and data
  • 85% gross margins provide pricing flexibility
  • Value-based pricing tied to outcomes, not features
  • Multiple tiers capture different willingness to pay
